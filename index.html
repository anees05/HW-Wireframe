Club Bio

Manchester United Football Club is a professional English football club based in Greater Manchester. The club competes in the Premier League, which is the top flight of English Football. The club's Nickname is "the Red Devils", and was founded as Newton Heath LYR Football Club in 1878. They later changed the name to Manchester United in 1902 and moved into the famous stadium, Old Trafford, in 1910. Having won the more trophies than any other English club, Manchester United is known around the world as one of the most prestigious clubs.
